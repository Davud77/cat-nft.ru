Welcome to TON NFT CAT

TON CAT NFT is 9.999 NFT cats created on The Open Network.

What is TON NFT CAT and why buy them?

Our team is the first to make an NFT collection of cats for TON. Cats will change history and in the future we will see a massive exodus of NFTs in TON! List of participants who have already joined our feline ranks


Why TON and not Ethereum?

We have been watching the development of TON for a very long time and were pleasantly surprised at how fast the infrastructure around the network is developing. We were also impressed by the opportunity to be among the first NFT collections, and even the opportunity to set high standards for future collections in TON. Soon you will see for yourself, we will try to share with you as often as possible!


How will NFTs be distributed?

The total NFT supply will be 9,999 unique NFTs. 1,111 of which will be sold out in the telegram community
